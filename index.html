Solar System

What is the Solar System?

The solar system is a stellar system that includes the Sun and all the astronomical objects gravitationally bound to it. These objects include planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other celestial bodies. The solar system formed from a cloud of gas and dust in interstellar space that collapsed under the influence of gravity about 4.6 billion years ago.
The solar system serves as a typical planetary system and is fundamental to our understanding of the cosmos and space exploration.
